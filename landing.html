Features

Everything you need to
run a profitable flip

Three powerful tools, one platform. From the moment you submit a bid to the day you close the sale.

📋
Bid & Contractor Portal
Send contractors a simple link to submit bids with full labor and materials breakdowns. Review, approve, reject, or request revisions — all in one queue. Nothing hits your actuals until you approve it.
💵
Expense Reimbursement
Partners at a hardware store can snap a photo of their receipt and submit it in 30 seconds. Track who paid, who's been reimbursed, and what's still outstanding — per person and per project.
📊
Project P&L Dashboard
Flip-specific profit and loss with estimated vs actual for every cost category. Labor and materials auto-populate from approved bids. See your ROI, margin, and net profit in real time.
🏗️
Per-Project Dashboards
Every project gets its own dashboard with 4 tabs: P&L, Bids, Expenses, and Charts. Filter by date range, view cost breakdowns, and track every dollar from purchase to sale.
👥
People & Subcontractors
See every contractor and partner in one place. Total bids, total expenses, what's been paid, what's outstanding — all in one expandable view across every project.
📈
Charts & Reporting
Visual cost breakdowns, ROI donuts, paid vs outstanding per person, and bid status charts. Filter everything by month, quarter, year, or custom date range. Export to Excel any time.
How it works

From bid to profit in three steps

1
Add your project
Create a project with the address and partners. This becomes the hub for all bids, expenses, and financial tracking for that flip.
2
Send the links
Give contractors the /bid link and partners the /expense link. They submit everything — you review and approve from the dashboard.
3
Watch the numbers
Approved bids and expenses automatically populate your P&L actuals. See your real-time profit, ROI, and outstanding reimbursements at a glance.
