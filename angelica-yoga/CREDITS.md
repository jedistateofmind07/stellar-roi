# Credits & Attribution

## Pose content

Pose names, traditional Sanskrit names, instructions, benefits, and contraindications are written from common Vinyasa yoga reference material. They are intended as a starting reference, not medical or professional teaching advice — practitioners should consult a qualified instructor and listen to their body.

## Pose images

The shipped app uses original SVG silhouette illustrations (one per category) created for this project. They are free for any use.

When real photos are swapped in (see `README.md`), record attribution here in this format:

```
- pose-id — Pose Name — [Source URL] — Photographer — License (e.g. CC-BY-SA 4.0)
```
